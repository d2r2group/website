The Data-Driven Renewables Research Group

The Data-Driven Renewables Research (D2R2 - Disney, please don’t sue) Group led by Dr. Peter Schindler is seeking to discover novel materials for renewable energy applications using high-throughput quantum chemistry calculations and data-driven materials property predictions.
